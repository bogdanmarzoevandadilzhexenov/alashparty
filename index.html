    Members of the Alash party were the largest in the government of Alash Orda. As you know, history is created by the people, laws and legal documents regulating the laws of historical development of society, political and legal doctrines are implemented by specific individuals, and the state language also has a great role in this area. We know from history that during the years when the Kazakh land was under the rule of the Russian self-government for more than two centuries, the Kazakh people were deprived of the right to rule their own destiny, a people with a unique political history dating back to the Blue Turkic era, completely blocked by Imperial laws.

    This tragedy of Kazakh society began to manifest itself in the advanced, open-minded representatives of the people. They led the people to a political struggle. The reason for this was the double revolution of 1917 in Russia. The fall of the Tsarist government from the throne further heated the political struggle. The Kazakh intelligentsia did not look back on the dangerous path of political activity, but distinguished personalities who selflessly fought. They were: Alikhan Bukeikhanov, Akhmet Baitursynov, Mirjaqip Dulatuli, Zhakyp Akbayev, Alimkhan Yermekov, Khalel Dosmukhamedov, Zhakhansha Dosmukhamedov, and others. They immediately realized that the Tsarist self-government was the greatest obstacle to the formation of political feelings of the Kazakh people, its development into a political struggle.

    Historical events related to the Alash party


    At the beginning of the last century, most of the territory of the Kazakhs became the property of the Russian state, the number of immigrants increased sharply, the indigenous population was forcibly displaced from the inhabited lands. The struggle against the policy of tsarism was led by the national intelligentsia, forming the Alash movement after the 1st Russian Revolution. The Karkaraly petition of 1905 became the program document of the Alash movement. At the end of 1905 in "delegate congress" of representatives of five regions of Kazakhstan was convened in Uralsk, at which a preliminary decision was made on the creation of the Kazakh national party, on the basis of which the Alash party later emerged

    After the October Revolution, the life of the people deteriorated, the political forces were scattered. Under these conditions, the leaders of the Kazakh liberal democratic movement, taking into account the rapidly changing revolutionary situation and the directly opposite goals of political forces, decided to speed up the holding of the all-Kazakh congress.
