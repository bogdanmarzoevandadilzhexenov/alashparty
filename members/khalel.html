Khalel Dosmukhamedov (1883-1939) (Kazakh: Халел Досмұхамедов) — state and public figure, member of the Government of Alashorda, the first Kazakh professor of mathematics. The Argyn tribe comes from the yellow Tanas branch of the Karakesek tribe.

Brief information
Early life
Political life



He was born on April 24, 1883 in the village of No. 4 on the sands of Taisoygan, Guryev uyezd, Taisoygan volost, former Ural region. He came from the Berish tribe of the baiuly tribe. Under the influence of his brother Dauletumbet Mashakuly, his father Dosmukhamed, who received a letter from a village Mullah, sends Khalel to the local Russian-Kazakh school. After graduating from high school, Khalel was enrolled in the preparatory class of the Ural military Real School in Teke in 1894, where he graduated with honors and was left to study in an additional class for another year.

Then he entered the St. Petersburg Imperial Military Medical Academy with an additional exam in Latin. Student life coincided with a period of political turmoil in the Empire, which influenced the development and formation of its political education. During these years, he campaigned throughout the country, wrote articles for local newspapers "Fikr" (opinion), "Uralsky listok", and explained the circumstances of political unrest to the public.




In 1917, he was elected to the Council of the nation — the government of Alash Orda, proclaimed at the II all-Kazakh Congress. Together with the Alash figures, he first made efforts to form the National Army — the people's militia, which protects the people from Rebellion, the creation of the Alash fund, and the collection of tribute funds from the country. When the rule of the Bolsheviks, who forcibly seized power from the Provisional Government, was strengthened, they held face-to-face negotiations with the head of the Soviet government, V. I. Lenin, and the people's commissar for national affairs, I. V. Stalin. When the Bolsheviks fully recognized the Soviet power and demanded unconditional submission, they put forward such important issues as the partial transfer of power to the Alashorda, the return to the Kazakhs of all places of concentration of Kazakhs, the release from prison of Alash figures arrested by the Soviet authorities everywhere, the cessation of their persecution, financial assistance.

In 1918, at the 4th congress of Kazakhs of the Ural region, held in Zhympity, he took part in the proclamation of the "Provisional Government of the UIL ualayaty"-a national territorial structure that would influence the entire western region of Kazakhstan. In mid-September of the same year, alashorda leaders, who met at the meeting to declare the Directorate of anti-Soviet forces, supported this structure and gave it the name "Western Branch of Alashorda". During this period, Khalel contributed a lot to organizing the cavalry of Alashorda, obtaining weapons from the Komuch government in Samara, opening a National Bank, publishing a printing house, and publishing a newspaper "Erkin Kazak". After the dissolution of the Alashorda, Khalel, like other Kazakh intellectuals, will be involved in the work of the new government.
